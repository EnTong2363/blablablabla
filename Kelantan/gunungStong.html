Gunung Stong State Park

"Gunung" means mountain in the local language, and Gunung Stong State Park is home to numerous peaks, rivers, and caves. Managed by the Kelantan State Forestry Department, this pristine forest reserve spans over 20,000 hectares. Visitors can explore mountain peaks like Gunung Tera, Gunung Ayam, and Gunung Stong, as well as the Jelawang Waterfall, the highest in Southeast Asia. The dense forest also shelters wildlife like tigers, bears, and elephants, along with exotic birds such as hornbills. Outdoor enthusiasts will enjoy jungle trekking, mountain climbing, and other exciting activities.
