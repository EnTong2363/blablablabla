About Zoo Taiping & Night Safari

Explore the captivating wildlife at Malaysia's oldest zoo, Zoo Taiping & Night Safari, situated in Perak. Covering more than 36 acres, Zoo Taiping houses over 140 different species. Established in 1961, just a few years after the nation's independence, it holds the distinction of being the country's first zoo.
