About The Huskitory

My fascination with Siberian Huskies began in my youth, and adopting my first Husky, Sugar, in 2010 was a dream come true. They say you can't just have one Husky, and that proved true for me. As my collection of Huskies grew, I created The Huskitory, a special place dedicated to my passion.


Establishing The Huskitory was a pivotal moment, allowing me to leave my previous career and fully commit to sharing my love for Huskies. Here, we don't just showcase these magnificent dogs but also spread knowledge, joy, and hope to visitors from around the world. The Huskitory has become more than a gallery; it's a home that bridges the gap between people and dogs, offering a harmonious blend of nature and humanity.
