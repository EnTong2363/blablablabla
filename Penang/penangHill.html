Penang Hill

If you're looking to escape Malaysia's tropical heat and enjoy a refreshing atmosphere, Penang Hill is the perfect destination. This charming hill station on Penang Island offers breathtaking views, lush jungle trails, and a collection of mountain peaks. It's home to the longest funicular railway track in Asia, making it a popular spot for trekking, hiking, train rides, and sightseeing. The funicular train ride is the highlight for most visitors, making Penang Hill a must-visit to create lasting memories.
