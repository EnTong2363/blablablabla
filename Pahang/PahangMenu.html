Pahang

Pahang, the largest state in Peninsular Malaysia, is predominantly covered in lush rainforest, offering a haven of greenery, exotic wildlife, and natural wonders. Its attractions include Taman Negara, a 130-million-year-old rainforest, along with parks, lakes, waterfalls, and sanctuaries. Other highlights are Endau-Rompin State Park and the serene Tioman Island. The capital, Kuantan, is a lively city with much to see. Visit Pahang and experience this natural paradise!
