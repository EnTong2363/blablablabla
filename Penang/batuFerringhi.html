Batu Ferringhi Beach

Batu Ferringhi Beach in Penang is a must-visit for sunbathing enthusiasts, offering golden sands and a shining sun that draw travelers back repeatedly. This beach is perfect for a relaxing holiday, with its serene views providing a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle. In addition to sunbathing, visitors can enjoy swimming and fishing. The beach's sunset views are breathtaking, making an overnight stay even more memorable.
