About

Lost World of Tambun offers an exciting and family-friendly adventure with lush jungles, ancient limestone formations, natural hot springs, and seven unique attraction parks. It's an ideal day-and-night eco-adventure spot for all ages, with the Lost World Hotel nearby for a restful stay after a day of exploration.
