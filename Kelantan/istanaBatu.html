Istana Batu

Istana Batu, located in Kelantan, Malaysia, was originally built as a wedding gift for Sultan Yahya Petra and Raja Perempuan Zainab II. Converted into a museum in 1991, it still exudes a homely atmosphere, showcasing personal belongings of the royal family alongside exhibits of silverware, glassware, Thai porcelain, and an array of gifts received by the royals, including Japanese dolls and Chinese tea sets.
