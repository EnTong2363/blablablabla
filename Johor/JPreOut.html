About Johor Premium Outlets

Johor Premium Outlets, located in Johor Bahru, is Southeast Asia's first luxury premium outlet mall. Spanning two floors and 45 acres, it features high-end fashion, jewelry, and home furnishings on the first and second floors, respectively. The ground floor offers a gourmet food court with various dining options. Its stunning architecture and prime location make it easily accessible from both Johor and Singapore.
