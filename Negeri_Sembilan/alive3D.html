Alive 3D Art Gallery

Alive 3D Art Gallery in Port Dickson is a unique attraction featuring 50 interactive paintings and optical illusions that will both amuse and captivate you. The gallery is divided into five themed zones: Adventure, Aquarium, Animation, Exotic, and Dark. Each zone is designed to trick your eyes, offering a mind-blowing experience and the opportunity to capture stunning 3D-style photos that will leave others in awe!
