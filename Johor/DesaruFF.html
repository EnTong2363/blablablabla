About Desaru Fruit Farm

180 Acres of Tropical Fruitland

Desaru Fruit Farm, recognized as the “Best Agro-Tourism Site” in Malaysia (2006) and awarded for Innovative Tourism Attraction - Agro Tourism Conservation (2010), offers a wealth of experiences. Explore GOOD Agricultural Practices (GAP) and Integrated Pest Management (IPM) in our Agriculture Gallery. Visit the Herbal & Vegetable Garden, attend an Educational Beekeeping talk, and taste raw honey. Don't miss the Mini Mart for local products and fresh fruits.


Discover The Wonders Through Your 5 Senses

The journey began with a guided tour of 180 acres of tropical fruit land, including visits to the agricultural gallery, fish pond, and vegetable and herb garden. Children enjoyed the petting zoo and an educational talk on bees, with the chance to taste local raw honey. Breakfast, lunch, and dinner were all provided.
