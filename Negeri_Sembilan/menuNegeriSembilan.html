Negeri Sembilan

Malaysia is a country full of beauty at every turn, and Negeri Sembilan, a state on the western coast of Peninsular Malaysia, is no exception. Although smaller and less known than tourist hotspots like Melaka or Penang, Negeri Sembilan offers numerous must-see attractions and activities. The name "Sembilan," meaning "nine," symbolizes its status as the ninth state. From the stunning Port Dickson beaches to recreational forests and even an ostrich farm, each natural landscape and beach here is sure to impress.
