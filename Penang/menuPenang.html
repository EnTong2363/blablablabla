Penang

Penang Island, known as Pulau Pinang in Malay, is a top travel destination for both Malaysians and international tourists. Recognized by CNN as one of the 22 best places to visit in 2023, the island offers a rich blend of culture, heritage, and natural beauty. Visitors can explore a variety of attractions, from lush tropical jungles and exotic beaches to historic landmarks and modern architecture. Penang is also home to stunning temples, mosques, and churches, showcasing its harmonious blend of cultural and religious traditions.
