About of Colmar Tropicale French Village

Colmar Tropicale French Village is an ideal starting point for your adventure in Bukit Tinggi. This charming area replicates the picturesque town of Colmar in Alsace, France, featuring vibrant shophouses and brick towers. The architecture is authentically inspired by the original Colmar.

Japanese Village at Colmar Tropicale

The Japanese Village, perched on a hilltop at Berjaya Hills in Bukit Tinggi, offers a serene Japan-themed experience just five minutes from Colmar Tropicale Resort. The village features a Japanese Tea House, Botanical Garden, Tatami Spa, and more. Visitors can enjoy rock pools, koi ponds, flower gardens, and traditional tea ceremonies. Surrounded by rainforest, it's also a popular spot for bird watching with scenic valley views along the trails.
