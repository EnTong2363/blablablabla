About

Resorts World Genting (RWG), Malaysia's top integrated resort destination, offers approximately 10,500 rooms across seven distinct hotels. The resort features a wide range of leisure and entertainment options, including gaming, theme park attractions, dining, retail outlets, international shows, and business convention facilities. Situated in the mid-hills, Resorts World Awana adds to the premium experience with its natural surroundings, mountainous golf course, trekking trails, and close proximity to key attractions. Additionally, the Genting Highlands Premium Outlets, a collaboration between Genting Plantations Berhad and Simon Property Group, enhances RWG's diverse offerings and reinforces its position as a leading leisure and entertainment hub in the region.

Genting Skyway

Covering 3.38 km in just 15 minutes, the Genting SkyWay transports you from Gohtong Jaya Station to Highlands Station at the summit. During the ride, you'll enjoy stunning views of a historic 130-million-year-old rainforest and the hilly terrain of Genting Highlands.

First World Plaza

First World Plaza, located at the Genting Resort and adjacent to SkyAvenue at the peak, is a shopping mall with numerous tourist attractions such as Skytropolis Funland, Snow World, and Senikome Peng Heng. It also features over 30 retail outlets, along with various restaurants and cafes. The mall is open daily from 10 am to 10 pm.
