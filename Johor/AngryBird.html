About Angry Birds Activity Park

Located in the Komtar JBCC Mall, this vibrant and one-of-a-kind destination will bring your children's Angry Birds fantasies to life! Spanning an area of 26,000 square feet, this air-conditioned indoor playground is the first Angry Birds theme park in Southeast Asia, offering a wide range of exciting activities for guests of all ages, from toddlers to kids and the young at heart.
