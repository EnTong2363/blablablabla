P.D Ostrich Show Farm

PD Ostrich Show Farm in Negeri Sembilan is a must-visit destination. The highlight is the friendly ostriches that greet visitors upon arrival. Beyond ostriches, the farm is like a small zoo, home to horses, ponies, peacocks, geese, donkeys, turkeys, and rabbits. Visitors can feed the animals, enjoy ostrich or pony rides, and sample ostrich satay at the on-site cafeteria.
