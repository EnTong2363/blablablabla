Gunung Datuk Recreational Forest

If hiking is your passion, Gunung Datuk Recreational Forest is a must-visit. The climb is physically demanding, taking nearly two hours to reach the 884-meter summit, but the effort is well worth it. At the top, you'll be rewarded with breathtaking views and a refreshing breeze. A long ladder helps you access the peak, and camping facilities are available with tent rentals for just RM 3.00. The hike down is easier and quicker, allowing you to soak in the stunning natural scenery on your descent.
