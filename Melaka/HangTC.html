About hang Tuah Centre

The reference center celebrates Hang Tuah's legacy as an admirable figure, showcasing his life journey from childhood to adolescence, based on Malay history and the Hikayat Hang Tuah. With interactive displays and modeling techniques, the visit is engaging and informative. The complex also includes a mini auditorium for theater, film screenings, cultural performances, and seminars. It features five houses representing Malay warriors—Hang Tuah, Hang Jebat, Hang Kasturi, Hang Lekir, and Hang Lekiu—who served the Melaka government. These houses highlight the excellence of Malay architecture, rich with symbolic meaning, and each is equipped with a silat court for activities like silat training and cultural events.
