About

Teluk Cempedak is the most popular beach in Kuantan, known for its picturesque bay with a broad, sandy shoreline and a breezy sea. Located just 5 km from the city center, it attracts both locals and tourists with its array of shops, hawker stalls, fast food outlets, and restaurants, offering ample facilities.


At the west end, a short boardwalk leads to Teluk Tongkang, a secluded beach nestled in a bay. Visitors can also hike up Bukit Pelindung from a trailhead along the boardwalk. The area remains lively at night, with locals enjoying dining, shopping, and amusement park activities. Nearby, across from Teluk Cempedak along the main road, Hoi Yin kopitiam is famous for its curry laksa.

Cherating Fire Fly

Cherating Fire Fly offers tours in Cherating, Kuantan, Pahang, to explore the stunning mangrove forests and watch the renowned fireflies. To book your Cherating River Cruise or Fireflies Watching Trip, simply email us, and we'll arrange your tour. The Cherating River Cruise is available at 9 am or 4 pm and lasts 90 minutes. Firefly Watching Trips are held daily at 7:30 pm and 8:30 pm, except Mondays, and last 60 minutes.

Turtle Hatching

From May to September, the Chendor Turtle Sanctuary allows visitors to see green turtles and occasionally giant leatherbacks nesting. Managed by the Fisheries Department, it includes an information center, souvenir kiosk, and turtle pools. Located near Cherating, it is a top spot for turtle watching.
