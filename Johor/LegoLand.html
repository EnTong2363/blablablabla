About LEGOLAND® Malaysia Resort

LEGOLAND Malaysia Resort features the best rides, slides, shows, and attractions from LEGOLAND Parks worldwide, customized for the local environment. Spanning an area larger than 50 football pitches, it houses over 15,000 LEGO models made from 60 million LEGO bricks. Other LEGOLAND Resorts are in Japan, the UAE, Denmark, Germany, the US, and the UK.


WHERE IMAGINATIONS COME TO LIFE

For a family day full of fun, visit LEGOLAND® Malaysia Resort, the ultimate destination for imagination! Whether earning your first driving license at LEGO® City Driving School, riding a Dragon through a medieval castle, or battling skeletons in LEGO NINJAGO®, the resort offers over 80 rides, live shows, and attractions—where imaginations come to life.
