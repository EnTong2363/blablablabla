About The Shore Sky Tower

Rising above the heart of Melaka, The Shore Sky Tower delivers an unparalleled sky-walk adventure 43 floors up. As the tallest man-made structure in Malaysia with a full 360-degree panoramic view, this observation deck allows visitors to take in the unique mix of the historical and modern aspects of the city.
