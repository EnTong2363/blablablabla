About

Cameron Highlands, a serene collection of townships situated nearly 2000 meters above sea level, rests amid tranquil mountains. Its cool resorts, tea plantations, and strawberry farms are spread across lush valleys and rolling hills, creating an ideal setting for a relaxing retreat. The region's peaceful atmosphere, beautiful landscapes, and cool climate offer a unique experience for visitors. Known for its fertile mountain slopes, Cameron Highlands has a mild climate with daytime temperatures around 25°C and cooler nights at 18°C, making it perfect for tea and vegetable cultivation. Located in Pahang, Cameron Highlands is a 4-5 hour drive from Kuala Lumpur, Penang, and Taman Negara National Park.

Mossy Forest

In addition to its vast tea plantations and strawberry farms, Cameron Highlands boasts several hidden treasures. One notable gem is the captivating Mossy Forest, a uniquely beautiful forest nestled on Mount Brinchang, the second highest peak in the region. At an altitude of 2,032 meters, this mystical forest is enveloped in mist and exudes a magical atmosphere.
