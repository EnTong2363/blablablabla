KOMTAR Penang

For a stunning bird's-eye view of Penang, KOMTAR Penang is a must-visit. This 65-storey tower, one of the island's most iconic landmarks, is located in central Georgetown. Similar to New York's Empire State Building, KOMTAR houses the state government's offices and features an exotic rooftop view. The lower floors are bustling with boutiques and electronic shops, while the top floors are dedicated to offices. Visitors can explore various stores, including jewelry and souvenir shops, and enjoy popular eateries like KFC and McDonald's. The rooftop deck, offering panoramic views, is accessible for RM 5.00 from 10:00 am to 10:30 pm, though it's closed during rain. The morning is ideal for capturing iconic photos.
