Explore Kedah

Discover the natural wonders and diverse cultures of Kedah, Malaysia. Enjoy the breathtaking landscapes and vibrant traditions of this unique state.

What About Kedah

Kedah, located in the northwest of Peninsular Malaysia, is known as the "Rice Bowl of Malaysia" due to its vast paddy fields. The state is rich in history, culture, and natural beauty, making it a perfect destination for those seeking a blend of tradition and tranquility.

Kedah is a paradise for nature lovers and history buffs alike. Whether you're exploring the ancient ruins of Lembah Bujang or enjoying the serene beaches of Langkawi, Kedah offers a diverse range of experiences for visitors.

The people of Kedah are known for their warm hospitality and deep-rooted traditions. Visitors can immerse themselves in local culture through the state's unique festivals, traditional crafts, and local delicacies.

One of Kedah's highlights is Gunung Jerai, a mountain that offers stunning panoramic views and is a popular spot for hikers. Additionally, the Lembah Bujang Archaeological Museum provides insight into Kedah's ancient history as one of Southeast Asia's oldest civilizations.

Attractions

Langkawi

Langkawi is a stunning archipelago off the coast of Kedah, known for its beautiful beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush tropical landscapes. It is a popular destination for both relaxation and adventure.

Gunung Jerai

Gunung Jerai, a prominent mountain in Kedah, offers breathtaking views and a cool, refreshing climate. It's a popular spot for nature lovers, hikers, and those looking to escape the tropical heat.

Gunung Keriang

The Limestone Caves of Kedah are a natural wonder, featuring stunning stalactites and stalagmites. These caves are also home to a variety of wildlife and offer a unique adventure for explorers.

Lembah Bujang

Lembah Bujang is an archaeological site that dates back over 1,500 years. It is home to ancient ruins and artifacts from one of Southeast Asia's earliest civilizations, offering a glimpse into Kedah's rich history.
